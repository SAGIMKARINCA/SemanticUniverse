# SUH-20260330-02 | Locale redirect flow was stabilized for history and sources

Date: 2026-03-30

Day Order: 2

Category: History

## What Was Discussed
- We evaluated the observation that multilingual switching was behaving inconsistently on the history and sources pages.
- It became clear that the problem might exist not only in content files, but also in the language-switch redirect flow.

## What Was Done
- We reviewed the redirect flow that was carrying the previous `lang` query parameter.
- The route logic was updated so shared view data now produces a clean redirect URL.
- The fix was recorded in shared memory as both a decision and a timeline entry.

## Why It Was Done
- To prevent the previous query parameter from overriding the newly selected language.
- To make the multilingual experience on the history and sources layers stable and repeatable.

## Outcome
- The language switcher now works with a clean redirect URL.
- Locale transitions on the history and sources pages were moved to a more reliable structure.
