# SUH-20260330-01 | The history and sources content layer became locale-based

Date: 2026-03-30
Day Order: 1
Category: History

## What Was Discussed
- It was observed that language switching worked in the main shell, but did not yet change the expected content inside history and sources.
- It was clarified that the history layer had to load timeline, decisions, definitions and experiments from locale-based files.
- It became clear that source cards also needed English category, summary, role and status-note fields.

## What Was Done
- English `timeline`, `decisions`, `definitions` and `experiments` files were added for history.
- An English metadata layer was added to the sources manifest.
- Journal routes were completed so that they resolve content files by locale.

## Why It Was Done
- To prevent language switching from remaining only in the menu and shell.
- To establish a genuinely multilingual experience on the history and sources surfaces.

## Outcome
- Multilingual switching now works at the visible-content level in the history and sources pages.
